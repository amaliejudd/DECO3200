Use a keep cup

Using a keep cup reduces waste and has a positive impact on the environment around us. By choosing to reduce your waste, you are lowering carbon emissions from waste management and limiting plastic pollution.

Use a disposable cup

Using a disposable cup creates waste and has a negative impact on the environment around us. By choosing to use these, you are creating carbon emissions through waste management, and also creating plastic pollution.
